Shiftlimits

The original Shiftlimits website was built around a forum for young animators who self-taught together through the use of stick figures. The main website included a section for members of the inhouse team to upload and display their work, as well as a section for any registered member to upload and share their work piecemeal.

I began working with with the website owner to develop his designs around a PhpBB core for version 4 of the website onwards. I took ownership of the site in late 2008 and closed the website as an animation community in 2010.

In 2020 I began releasing my open source work under the name ShiftLimits.
