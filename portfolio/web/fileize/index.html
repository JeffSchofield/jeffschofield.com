Fileize

Fileize was a multi-purpose file host that I built and operated for a community of artists and animators. The focus of the service was to give members of the community a way to store and share their work, as well as facilitate quick transfer of project files between collaborators.

The website was sold in 2012 and is no longer online as of 2013.
