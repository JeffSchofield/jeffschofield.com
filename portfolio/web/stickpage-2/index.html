StickPage 2.0

While working for Max Games I was tasked with rebuilding the StickPage website with features similar to that of projects I had worked on in the past. This project was my first major foray away from PHP into NodeJS and eventually Vue on the frontend.

The project was cancelled at the start of 2018 due to the landscape for internet animation changing drastically from the start of the decade. The company decided to primarily focus on mobile games.

Key Features

  • Flash animation and games portal with shared authorship, community ranking, commenting, and related media collaborative filtering
  • Feature system for content moderators to highlight community works on the front page
  • Content browser with multi-faceted search and filtering
  • Sponsored content system to compensate talented content creators that post their work
