Baker’s Dozen

Baker’s Dozen is an endless defense game with brawler elements. Enemies enter your bakery from the right at a steady rate and run toward your cake to steal it. You have to lay traps between waves and use your brawling skills to keep the thieves away.

I built this game with a friend while we were in highschool. I did the programming as well as some animation. It was my first flash game to get a sponsorship online.
