About Jeff Schofield

Hello! Welcome to the personal website of Jeff Schofield. I am a soft­ware designer and developer from Peterborough, Ontario who special­izes in application develop­ment for the web. I have 11 years of professional experience designing and con­structing websites, web applications, as well as the server software and system infra­structure that powers it all.

I am currently working with local telecom, Nexicom, toward a private cloud-native enterprise architecture running a suite of purpose-built web applications for our staff and our customers.

We are using technologies such as Kubernetes, Terraform, and ArgoCD to deploy and manage infrastructure and application containers based on GitOps principles. I am involved in every part of the stack, from the design system and UI library, through Vue-based frontend clients and servers, to the NestJS-powered GraphQL API and databases that carry out our business logic.

I also publish my open source work as ShiftLimits on GitHub, where I have a suite of frontend design tooling that I use across my own work available for anyone to use.

I like working on unique projects. Check out my work, my personal GitHub, and email me or get in touch through my LinkedIn if you would like to work together.
